About SkinTone.ai

SkinTone.ai is an AI-powered application designed to help users classify their skin tone into four categories: Light, Mid Light, Dark, and Mid Dark.

The application uses a deep learning model trained on a curated dataset of images, ensuring accurate and reliable skin tone classification.

By understanding your skin tone, SkinTone.ai provides personalized color recommendations to help you choose outfits that complement your natural look and enhance your confidence.

How It Works

  • Upload a photo or take a picture directly in the app.
  • The AI model analyzes the image and classifies your skin tone.
  • Receive outfit color suggestions tailored to your skin tone.

Benefits

  • Boosts confidence with personalized fashion tips.
  • Simple and easy-to-use interface.
  • Powered by advanced machine learning for accurate classification.
